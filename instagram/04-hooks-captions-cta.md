# Swipe File — Hooks, Captions, CTAs & Lead Magnet

Grab-and-go copy. All written in your audience's plain, no-nonsense voice.

---

## Hooks (first line / first 2 seconds)

The hook is ~80% of the job. Lead with the boldest one on screen *before* you speak.

**Pain hooks (Pillar B):**
- "That lead just cost you £280."
- "Google your own business right now. I'll wait."
- "Most tradesmen are paying for leads they'll never win."
- "Your website is pretty. Nobody can find it."
- "If you're not top 3 on Google, half your customers never see you."
- "Checkatrade isn't a marketing strategy. It's a tax."

**Curiosity / attention hooks (Pillar A):**
- "You'll recognise all of these in 2 seconds. (You won't recognise the last one.)"
- "Why does everyone know McDonald's but nobody knows your business?"
- "This one logo makes billions. Yours could too — locally."
- "The reason whoever's top 3 on Google is winning your jobs."

**Authority / brand hooks (Pillar C):**
- "Most agencies marketing to trades have never been on a site."
- "Here's what 'top 3 on Google' is actually worth in £."
- "We don't sell leads. We make Google send you free ones."

## Caption formula

1. **Re-hook** (don't repeat the on-screen text — extend it).
2. **One sting** (the stat, made painful).
3. **One CTA** ("Comment RANK…").
4. **3–5 hashtags** (1 broad + 2–3 niche + 1 branded).

**Front-load the keyword in the first 125 characters** — captions are SEO. Start captions with phrases like "Google Business Profile for tradesmen…" / "Local SEO for [trade]…".

**Hashtag set (rotate, never more than 5):**
`#Tradesman #LocalSEO #GoogleBusinessProfile #Checkatrade #TrowelAndCode`
Trade-specific swaps: `#Plumber #Electrician #Builder #Roofer #Landscaper #HVAC`

## CTAs (one per post)

**Primary — DM keyword funnel (converts ~5–15% vs ~1–3% for bio links):**
- "Comment **RANK** and I'll send you the free checklist."
- "Comment **AUDIT** for a free look at where your Google profile is losing you calls."

**Brand/awareness posts:** "Follow for next week's Household Name."
**Engagement:** "Tag the mate whose business is invisible on Google."
**Soft service:** "Comment RANK for a free 15-min ranking review — no pitch, just where you stand."

> Keep CTA *text* out of the bottom 15% of the video frame (the IG interface covers it).

## The DM funnel (no paid leads, value-first)

1. Reel/carousel ends with **"Comment RANK."**
2. They comment → you (or an auto-DM tool) reply with the **free checklist** + one warm line: *"Here you go 👊 Want me to take a quick look at where you actually rank right now?"*
3. If yes → ask their business name + town, do a 2-minute look, send back 1–2 honest findings (give value, build trust).
4. Offer the **free 15-min ranking review** call. No hard pitch — the gap between where they are and top-3 sells itself.
5. Log every conversation. Track checklist requests → reviews booked → clients won.

## Lead magnet outline — "7 Things Knocking You Out of Google's Top 3"

A one-page PDF (or carousel). Each = a quick, real fix so it actually helps even if they never hire you (that's what earns trust):

1. **Incomplete Google Business Profile.** (Complete profiles get ~5x more views.) Fill every field.
2. **No reviews / not replying to them.** Ask every happy customer; reply to all.
3. **Wrong or missing service area / categories.** Google can't rank you for jobs it doesn't know you do.
4. **Website Google can't read** — no location, no trade keywords, slow on mobile.
5. **Inconsistent name/address/phone** across the web (Google trusts consistency — the McDonald's lesson).
6. **No photos / stale profile.** Fresh job photos signal an active, real business.
7. **Paying for lead-gen instead of building the asset you own.** The mindset fix.

**Last line:** *"Stuck on any of these? Comment RANK or DM us — we do this for trades all day."*

## Quick do / don't

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Plain English, foreman voice | Agency jargon ("leverage," "solutions") |
| Hook in 2 seconds | Slow logo intros |
| One CTA per post | Five asks at once |
| 3–5 hashtags | 30 hashtags |
| Optimise for shares + saves | Chase likes/follower count |
| Obscured/abstract logos in the quiz | Clean real brand logos (legal risk) |
| Proof: screenshots, real numbers | Stock photos, vague claims |
