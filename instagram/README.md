# Trowel and Code — Instagram Marketing Campaign

A brand-recognition campaign for Instagram, built on market research into how trades-focused agencies grow, how the 2026 algorithm rewards content, and what actually moves trades business owners.

**The core idea:** make tradespeople feel the pain of being invisible on Google, prove we fix it, and never sound like the lead-gen companies that burned them — *"We don't sell leads. We make Google send you free ones."*

## Things to act on before filming
- **Guess the brand → "guess the TOOL/TRADE brand"** (DeWalt, Makita, Screwfix by colour). Native to your audience, more shareable inside the trade, lower legal risk. Make it a recurring weekly series.
- **Still abstract the logos** (colour blocks / silhouettes) — better quiz, safer.
- **Lean on satisfying real footage** (resin pours etc.) for reach, with a brand/pain line pinned on top.
- **Reply to every comment** — it's the single strongest (and free) growth lever in the data.

## The strategy in one line
Make tradespeople feel the pain of being invisible on Google, prove you fix it, and never sound like the lead-gen companies that burned them — then, once you're in the door, fix the rest of the tech they're missing.
